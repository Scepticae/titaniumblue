About the Company

TitaniumBlue is a small, upstart software development company based in Knoxville, Tn. The company focuses on developing for personal and small businesses. Custom websites, web applications, and other software solutions are offered for affordable prices. Collaborative tools are used to discuss the customers needs and demonstrate mock-ups before the development is started. Using these tools allows the customer to fine tune the look and feel before coding begins assuring the customer is getting every feature they need. If this design process sounds good, contact titaniumBlue to get started.
